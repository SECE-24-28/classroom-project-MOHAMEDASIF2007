About Me

I’m Mohamed Asif, an IT student and passionate full-stack developer who loves turning ideas into meaningful digital experiences. I aim to build solutions that genuinely help people in their daily lives. Over the years, I’ve developed projects—from smart attendance systems using facial recognition to campus platforms like blood donation management and StubaZaar.

I enjoy backend and frontend development, with experience in Java, Spring Boot, Python, OpenCV, MySQL, HTML, CSS, and MERN basics. I constantly explore new technologies and improve my problem-solving skills.

My goal is to become a developer who builds real-world solutions that create a positive impact and improve everyday life.
